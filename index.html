VaultX Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how VaultX Wallet Extension ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Wallet addresses and public keys
  • Transaction history (publicly available on blockchain)
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Browser version and basic device information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate cryptocurrency transactions
  • Provide VaultX wallet functionality and services
  • Improve our extension performance
  • Debug technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Your private keys and sensitive data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to VaultX servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we do collect.

Third-Party Services

VaultX may interact with blockchain networks and other third-party services. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your VaultX account and associated data
  • Export your wallet data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
